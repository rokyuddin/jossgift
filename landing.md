# JossGift – Landing Page Documentation

**Brand:** JossGift
**Tagline:** *Story with Chocolate • Emotion in Every Box*
**Market:** Bangladesh (Mobile-first)
**Primary Focus:** Premium chocolate gift boxes & corporate gifting

---

## 1. Landing Page Objective

The landing page is designed to sell **emotion and experience**, not just products. Its goals are to:

* Instantly communicate luxury, trust, and emotional value
* Make users feel this is a *premium gifting brand*, not a generic shop
* Guide visitors smoothly toward shopping without confusion
* Build confidence for first-time buyers in Bangladesh
* Be extremely easy to use for non-technical, mobile-first users

---

## 2. Overall UX & Visual Direction

### Look & Feel

* Premium, elegant, luxury-focused design
* Clean layout with generous spacing
* Neutral or dark base with chocolate/gold accent tones
* High-quality photography with soft lighting
* Subtle micro-animations (hover, fade-in, scroll reveal)

### UX Principles

* Mobile-first layouts (primary audience)
* One clear primary action per section
* Emotional storytelling over technical descriptions
* Minimal cognitive load for quick decision-making

---

## 3. Landing Page Structure (Top to Bottom)

---

## 3.1 Hero Section (Above the Fold)

### Purpose

Create an immediate emotional connection and clearly express the brand promise.

### Content

* **Headline:**
  *Not Just a Gift Box — It’s a Story with Chocolate*
* **Sub-headline:**
  Premium chocolate gift boxes crafted to express emotions, celebrate moments, and surprise hearts.
* **Primary CTA:** Shop Gifts
* **Secondary CTA:** Explore Occasions

### Visual Direction

* Full-width premium product image or lifestyle shot
* Soft lighting, elegant composition
* Subtle motion (slow fade or parallax)

### Mobile Notes

* Headline limited to 2 lines
* CTA buttons stacked vertically

---

## 3.2 Shop by Occasion

### Purpose

Help users quickly find relevant gifts without thinking too much.

### Occasion Categories

* Birthday
* Anniversary
* Proposal
* Valentine’s Day
* Eid
* Wedding
* Friendship
* Corporate Events

### Design Notes

* Card-based layout
* Minimal image + occasion label
* Rounded corners with soft shadow
* Tap or hover animation

### UX Behavior

* Horizontal scroll on mobile
* Grid layout on desktop

---

## 3.3 Best Sellers Section

### Purpose

Reduce decision fatigue and build trust through popularity.

### Content

* Section title: *Our Most Loved Gifts*
* 4–6 featured products

### Product Card Includes

* High-quality product image
* Product name
* Short emotional tagline
* Price
* View Details CTA

### Trust Enhancers

* Badges like “Most Gifted” or “Customer Favorite”
